Our Journey

Founded in 2015, Mulachap Ventures began as a small financial consultancy with a big vision - to make loan solutions accessible to all Kenyans.

What started as a modest operation with just three employees has grown into one of Nairobi's most trusted financial services providers.

Over the years, we've helped over 5,000 clients secure financing totaling over KSh 500 million in loans.

Meet Our Founder

Immaculate Wangari Kimondo

Founder & CEO

With over 15 years in the financial sector, Immaculate founded Mulachap Ventures to address the growing need for accessible business financing in Kenya's SME sector.

Her expertise in risk assessment and micro-financing has shaped our unique approach to lending.

"Financial solutions should empower, not burden. That's why we focus on sustainable lending that helps businesses grow."

Under her leadership, Mulachap Ventures has become a market leader in innovative SME financing solutions.
